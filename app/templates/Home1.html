Our Mission

More than half of Americans are targeted by online harassment. 
Our mission is to combat hate speech. We built this product to reduce racial bias and be able to mitigate the uncontrolled spread of hate in social media by supervising growing online content. We trained neural networks to recognize hate speech in order for you to assess whether hate speech is in a particular text or not.  

AI Overview

Our team created an AI that can assess whether a text contains hate speech. The AI will detect specific words in the text and predict either the presence or absence of hate speech conscious of the word context. Based on the existence of these words, the AI will assign it to either hate speech positive or negative.

Why Hate Speech Detection

Hate Speech is the act of having "abusive or threatening speech or writing that expresses prejudice against a particular group, especially on the basis of race, religion, or sexual orientation." Some of the most frequent victims of hate speech are teenagers, with 12% reporting they often encounter racist hate speech online. Our Hate Speech Detector will be able to find out whether or not your text has hate speech.
