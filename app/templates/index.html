                  Overview


          What the product is and how it works

We created an AI that based on specific words it detects in the written review, can provide a sentiment. There are two categories: positive and negative. The AI will detect certain words that have a negative connotation and words that have a positive connotation. Based on how much positive or negative is in the review, the AI will assign it to one of the two categories.

Why this product?

We created this AI to make it easier for people who use online shopping to find the general message and emotion of a specific review. This could be particularly useful if a review is too long to read. As online shopping is slowly becoming the entire future of shopping, our AI will become more prominent.
